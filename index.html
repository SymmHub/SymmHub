SymmHub

About

SymmHub is a platform for creating symmetry-based applications, and a small collection of such apps. It is authored by Vladimir Bulatov and Chaim Goodman-Strauss, with assistance from Scott Vorthmann.

Applications

  • Minimal Sample
  • SymPix - Symmetry from pictures
  • SymSim - symmetry from simulation of Gray-Scott PDE
  • SymSim - symmetry from simulations of Ginzburg-Landau PDE
  • SymChaos - symmetry from Chaotic Iterated Attractors

Under Development

  • Orbifold (general hyperbolic orbifolds)
  • Tests
